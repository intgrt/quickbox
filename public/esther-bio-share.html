Hello and Welcome

Hello and Welcome, I'm Esther Chu

I'd love to collaborate with you to help you find healing and clarity through counselling. I care deeply about meeting you wherever you are at and walking beside you on your journey.

My approach is holistic and culturally-sensitive. I am particularly attuned to the intersectionality of your life experiences. What that means is there are many aspects of ourselves and our life experiences that are interconnected and can influence how we see ourselves and how the world might categorize us. These include things like: age, sex, gender, skin colour, sexual orientation, ethnicity, faith, (dis)ability and social class, to name a few.

I am skilled at holding the big picture of what might be influencing you and your current situation. I love to help you feel empowered in your own life to make the changes that feel true and important for you.

Outside of the Therapy Room

I love to travel and explore new cultures. I volunteer in a leadership role as Director-at-Large for the BC Filipino Therapist Association. I offered emergency support to many clients and ran community group therapy sessions following the Vancouver Lapu Lapu tragedy.
